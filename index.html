Overview

I'm a third-year, second-semester undergraduate student in the Department of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) at Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University (MBSTU), Bangladesh. My coursework spans Data Structures and Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming, embedded systems, communication engineering, data communication, Digital Signal processing (DSP), web application development, databases, Artificial Intelligence, Operating Systems and theory of computation.

Most of my recent focus has gone into an Arduino-based walkie-talkie — a bidirectional wireless audio system built on NRF24L01+ modules — which pulls together concepts from three separate courses into a single hands-on build. It's currently at the wireless-audio debugging stage.

Outside of coursework, I'm working through a self-directed track in Cyber Security and web application development roadmap, aiming toward a near-term goal.
